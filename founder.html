Projects I've Built

Osmosis

Osmosis began as an idea to solve liquidity fragmentation across the Cosmos ecosystem. While other chains were building isolated AMMs, we envisioned a protocol that could unify liquidity across the entire interchain.

The technical challenge was immense‚Äîcreating customizable liquidity pools that could handle cross-chain assets while maintaining security and efficiency. We pioneered novel concepts like superfluid staking and concentrated liquidity that have since influenced the broader DeFi space.

What started as a technical experiment has grown into one of the largest DEXs in Cosmos, facilitating billions in trading volume and becoming the liquidity hub for the Internet of Blockchains.

Key Milestones

  • 2021: Protocol launch and initial liquidity mining
  • 2022: Superfluid staking implementation
  • 2023: $1B+ in total value locked
  • 2024: Cross-chain expansion beyond Cosmos
